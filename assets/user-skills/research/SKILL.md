# Deep research

## When to use

The user wants **thorough investigation** and a durable write-up in the wiki, not a quick answer.

## Process

1. **Scope** — Restate the question and boundaries (time, geography, product area).
2. **Gather** — Use wiki read/search, email search/read, web search, and other tools as appropriate. Prefer primary sources.
3. **Synthesize** — Write one wiki page (or a structured addendum) with sections, links to related wiki pages, and clear attribution.
4. **Gaps** — Say what you could not verify.

## Quality bar

- One canonical output page with clear structure.
- Explicit separation of facts vs inference.
