# Project page template

Use for `**projects/<slug>.md**`. Sized for **recurring work** the user might ask about (“where are we on X?”), not every one-off task.

## Objective

- **Goal / scope** in one short paragraph — what “done” roughly means.

## Status

- **Current state:** *(active, paused, blocked, shipped — as of …)*
- **Next move or open decision** *(optional, when known)*

## People

- `**[[people/…]]`** wikilinks for owners, key partners, stakeholders *(when evidenced).*

## Topics / links

- `**[[topics/…]]*`* for durable themes tied to this work.
- External links only when stable and useful.

## Facts vs narrative

- Prefer **bullets** for decisions, dates, and constraints; keep prose short.
