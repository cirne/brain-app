# People

One page per person you track for mail, calendar, and chat. **Files:** kebab-case under `people/` (e.g. `people/jane-doe.md`). Link from `**[[me]]`** → Key people when useful.

Read `**[[people/template]]**` before `**write**` a new person page. Put each `**[[people/slug]]**` under **one** category below—move links if someone’s role in your life changes.

## Family

*(Parents, partner, kids, close relatives — links appear here as you add pages.)*

## Friends

*(Social, non-work closeness.)*

## Coworkers

*(Same org / team; people you work with day to day.)*

## Colleagues

*(Broader professional: other companies, clients, vendors, industry peers, collaborators outside your immediate team.)*
