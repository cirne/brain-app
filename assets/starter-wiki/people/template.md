# Person page template

Use this shape for `**people/<slug>.md**`. **Ground facts in mail, messages, or things the user said** — do not invent phone numbers, roles, or relationships.

## Overview

- **One or two lines:** who they are in *your* world from **actual message content** — role, topics, how you interact. **Not** contact rank, volume, or “prominent / frequently mentioned in mail” filler (those stats are not a bio).

## Relationship / context

- **How you know them:** *(evidence-backed — aligns with **Family** / **Friends** / **Coworkers** / **Colleagues** on `[[people/index]]`.)*
- **What you coordinate on:** *(projects, domains — link to `[[projects/…]]` or `[[topics/…]]` when useful.)*

## Contact / identifiers

- **Email:** *(from signatures or headers when evidenced.)*
- **Phone:** *(E.164 only when it appears in tool output or mail — never guessed.)*
- **Other handles:** *(optional — chat ids, LinkedIn, etc., when evidenced.)*

## Notes for replies

- **Preferences, sensitivities, or standing topics** that affect how you write to them — short bullets.

## Links

- `**[[me]]`** — if they appear in your network section.
- `**[[projects/…]]`**, `**[[topics/…]]**`, org pages — when it saves repetition.
