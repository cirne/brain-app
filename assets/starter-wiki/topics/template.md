# Topic page template

Use for `**topics/<slug>.md**` when the idea is **navigable and recurring** (not a generic phrase or one-off).

## What this is

- **Definition** in one short paragraph — what belongs on this page vs elsewhere.

## Why it matters here

- **Tie to the user’s life** when known — link `**[[people/…]]`**, `**[[projects/…]]**`.

## Stable facts

- **Bullets** — durable reference material; avoid dumping full articles.

## Fleeting / time-bound

- If something is **only relevant briefly**, put it in `**notes/`** or a dated bullet with intent to remove.

## Links

- Cross-link hubs and related topics so search and browse stay coherent.
